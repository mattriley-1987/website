Rotary Council Award

Weston is very pleased to announce they have been kindly awarded a donation of £150 of which £100 will be used to pay for a Rescue Blanket (to enable us to rescue disabled or motor impared sailors) by rolling them onto the blanket and into the Rescue boats. The remaining £50 will be put towards hiring a portable accessible toilet for our catamaran event in October, which will include the Challenger fleet.

 

Slipway Announcement - Work COMPLETED March 2013

WORK STARTED 01/03/2013 - will take approx. 3 weeks and should be ready in time for the GRAND SLAM!

Weston Sailing Club is delighted to announce that it has successfully secured an Inspired Facilities -  £50,000 grant to extend and repair our Central Slipway.

We still have to wait for final planning approval, but the process and discussion so far indicate that we will be successful.

All going well the work will begin and finish in early to mid October this year.

Thanks go to all the GP committee members that have worked toward getting us this far.
