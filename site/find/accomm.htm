Camping

There is a field at Weston during some Open meetings for visitors to park thier cars, store / rig dinghies and camp in. PLEASE EMAIL TO FIND OUT IF THIS IS APPLICABLE FOR YOUR CHOSEN OPEN EVENT. The first 30 competitors to turn up will be allowed free use, but thereafter a charge of £1.50 for each competitor. The club facilities are locked including the dinghy storage pound after normal operating hours (bar close) but a 'event key' will be issued to visiting group to allow entry to the clubs facilities
