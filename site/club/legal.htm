WSC Child protection policy, self disclosure, reference documents and Good Practice Guide (PDF opens in new window)

This document contains WSC Child protection policy guidelines developed with the RYA to help the club to enable children and vulnerable adults to enjoy the sports of sailing, windsurfing and power boating in all their forms, in a safe environment.
