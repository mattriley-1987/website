Power Boat Courses.
The club has the aim of having as many members as possible who undertake the Safety boat driver duty to possess the RYA level 2 Powerboat certificate.

All our powerboat course are now finished for 2014.

Sail Training Courses 2014

WSC will be providing a series of four sail training courses during 2014. -

  • 28/29/30 May 2014 (beginners)
  • 11/12/13 August 2014 (improvers)
  • 27/28/29 August 2014 (improvers)

The cost of the courses will be as follows:

  Course fee Boat 'rental' Total
Adult (18+) Member £30 £15 £45
Cadet Member   £15 £0 £15
There are no course fees for the first course for new 2014 members. Subsequent courses in 2014 will attract the normal course fee.

Our courses are run entirely by volunteers. The primary purpose of the training courses is to improve members’ skills and knowledge, although WSC is authorised to award RYA certificates for levels I, II and III (Racing) to those participants who achieve the relevant standards. The training courses will make use of WSC’s Fevas, Picos, Toppers, Lasers and Visions but participants are also encouraged to use their own boats (at their own risk). Such boats must be fully insured. If you wish to attend one of these training courses then please complete the form below and send it to the WSC Dinghy Training Administrator at the above address. Please ensure that a cheque, made payable to “Weston Sailing Club”, in the appropriate amount is enclosed with your application form as WSC cannot reserve places until payment is received. Participants must be at least 8 years old at the start of the course.

Please use the Sail Training Course Booking PDF to register your interest

Please print out the Course Application Form, fill in your details, and send it to the Dinghy Training Administrator at the address on the form.

The training courses are to improve members’ skills and knowledge. RYA certificates for levels I, II and III (Racing) may be received by participants who achieve the relevant standards.

Dinghy's used by WSC include Fevas, Picos, Toppers, Laser and Enterprise but participants are also encouraged to use their own boats (at their own risk).

WSC cannot reserve places until payment is received. Applications will not be processed before 1 May 2014.

Any questions please email training@weston.org.uk
