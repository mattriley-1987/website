Youth Sailing

Youth Racer Series

Weston Sailing Club wants to encourage our youngest members to join in club activities and as there are a few parents already sailing regularly with children.

The Junior Regatta date is:

  • Saturday 7th September 2013 (Junior Regatta) SEE THE OPENS PAGE FOR MORE INFO

If you would like to Enter please complete the ENTRY FORM and send back.

We hope that a few more members will come sailing at Weston with young crews and that this will help to build a group of keen young sailors- they are the club's future.

Inquiries,  or to register interest reply or email Honsec@weston.org.uk

All training and sailing is open to all ages and a youth trophy is awarded to the best performing youth sailor in the slow handicap class.  There is only one discrete event for young sailors and that is the Junior Regatta on the 7th. September 2013 details of which can be found from the programme web page.

Please note the Open Day on June 30th when there will be opportunities to try out a variety of boats, and the members night the same day when there will be an opportunity to meet committee members and other members.  Free barbecue.
