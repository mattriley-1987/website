Youth Sailing - Youth Catamaran Sailing

  • Ages - 7 to 18 years
  • Levels - Beginners to Advanced

Junior and Youth sailing runs throughout the Weston Sailing season from April through to November. It is open to local children between the ages of 7 and 18. It is run by volunteers and is open to both beginners and more experienced sailors.

Junior sailing courses will follow the RYA Youth Sailing Scheme. Youth sailing will cover more advanced techniques such as trapezing, spinnaker handling and sail control under more demanding conditions.

2010 dates soon....

The BHCCA took their cat introduction day on the road for the first time in October last year and it was a resounding success.  Previously all the intro days had been held at the National Sailing Academy in Weymouth.

The catamaran introduction days give youngsters between 11 and 16 the opportunity of sailing a catamaran in teams of two people.  The day is structured with the youngsters having an on shore introduction to the boats.  The children are shown how to perform basic manoeuvres such as tacking, gybing, trapezing, spinnaker hoists and capsize drill.  The sailors then get on to the water and are followed closely by the coach boat who gives on the water instruction to each team.

After a couple of hours, the sailors return to the club for a quick lunch break.  The afternoon session will see the children rotated on other boats and they all get to try trapezing.  The afternoon session ends with a few races and then it is back to the shore late in the afternoon for the all important debriefing.

The BHCCA supply the coach free of charge. We also deliver and help rig the 6 boats and supply the trapeze harnesses.

The hosting club supply a rib for the coach, a room suitable for briefing & de-briefing and the usual club facilities of catering and changing rooms with hot showers.  Most importantly the club promote the event in their area.  This often involves contacting other local clubs and schools. 

A nominal fee of £15 is paid by the sailors for a very active day’s sailing.

Should you require more information please email me at nick@youthcatsailing.org.uk.

Nick Henson
BHCCA Youth Co-ordinator

Volunteers required

Having run several successful sailing days we are now looking for more adult volunteers to help run events. No experience is required to get involved but we particularly require people with instructor qualifications or an ability to undertake maintenance on club boats. Please email for more details.
