2014 Open Meetings

How Exciting was your boat last weekend? Ever thought of a Spitfire?

Looking for an exciting Cat suitable for Youth or Adults? Then the Spitfire is the boat for you.
They will be racing at this event. Why not come along and see for yourselves the thrills, fun and exciting sailing they offer for all who sail them.
The Spitfire is the RYA pathway multi hull so for anyone under 19 it´s a great choice as the next step after your Junior Boat.
Never sailed a multihull before? Don´t worry come along and discover what Spitfire sailing has to offer you.
To book your trail sail email ukyouthcat@gmail.com or come and talk to us to find out more.
We will endeavour to try to get you out before or after racing for a quick spin or book you in for another time if you require a longer trail.
