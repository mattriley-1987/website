DUTIES

Our sailing club is operated on a voluntary basis, all members of the committee, people in rescue boats, race officers, social helpers are all club members. In order to run the club we rely on our members taking their turn at helping to run the club.

When you join the club you are committing to 2-4 duties per year, we run racing throughout the year from January to December inc. We offer all our members the option to train in the rescue boats to RYA level 2, so they can safely patrol the club racing.

Once your membership has been accepted you will receive your membership card and a copy of the current yearbook which contains all the information you need to know to get the best out of your membership of Weston Sailing Club.
