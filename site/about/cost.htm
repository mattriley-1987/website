Boat rental

During scheduled club sailing, one can also pay a day sailing fee £10/session/person and rent one of our club boats (members prices), we have:

  • 4 Picos @ £5/session each
  • 2 Fevas @ £10/session each
  • 1 Laser @ £10/session
  • 1 Contender @ £10/session
  • RS Vision / Enterprise £20/session

Please ensure you complete the day sailing form, available here or at the club.
